ASSAM

The Assam floods have caused widespread devastation, displacing thousands and damaging homes, crops, and infrastructure. The relentless monsoon rains have led to overflowing rivers, submerging vast areas. Rescue and relief efforts are ongoing, but the situation remains critical as the floodwaters continue to rise, impacting both human lives and wildlife.

WAYANAD LANDSLIDES

The Wayanad landslide, triggered by heavy monsoon rains, caused significant destruction, burying homes and cutting off access to remote areas. Rescue operations faced challenges due to the difficult terrain and continuous rainfall. The disaster has led to loss of life, displacement, and severe damage to the region's infrastructure and environment.
